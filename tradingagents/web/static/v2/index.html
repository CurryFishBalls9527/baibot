BAIBOT · TELEMETRY · DESIGN EXPLORATION

Four ways to watch the bot
think out loud.

Today's TRADE view is a fixed 3-pane (tickets · chart · thesis) with the active variant chosen from a dropdown. The hot pain points: variant switching is a context-switch tax, and watching firings live feels passive — you have to notice the new row.

Each concept below picks a different metaphor. They are not mutually exclusive — pieces can compose. Treat this as a kit of moves, not a beauty contest. Scroll down or click a card.
01 COMMAND FLOOR Variants are vertical LANES, not a dropdown — each one a column with its own pulse, tickets fall into the lane as cards. ↳ variant selection · live watch 02 CONVEYOR Time is the spine. Past trades to the left of NOW; pre-trade pipeline (with confidence climbing) to the right. ↳ live watch · anticipation 03 SPOTLIGHT One trade owns the screen. Cinematic full-bleed chart with thesis on glass cards. New firings queue at the side; tap to swap focus. ↳ live watch · deep investigation 04 VERSUS Variants pinned side-by-side as living columns. Pick any two to race; everything else collapses to a strip. ↳ variant selection · head-to-head
